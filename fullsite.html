About Me

With over eight years of experience in the beauty industry, I specialize in creating looks that celebrate and enhance your natural features. My approach combines technical expertise with an intuitive understanding of skin tones, textures, and individual beauty.

Based in the heart of the city, I've had the privilege of working with diverse clients for weddings, editorial shoots, and special events. My mission is simple: to make every woman feel confident, radiant, and authentically herself.

Whether you're looking for a soft, natural glow or full glam transformation, I bring passion, precision, and a calming presence to every appointment.
